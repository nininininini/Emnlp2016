Abstract#==#We present a novel semi-supervised approach for sequence transduction and     
apply it to semantic parsing. The unsupervised component is based on a               
    
generative model in which latent sentences generate the unpaired logical      
forms.
We apply this                                                                       
   
method to a number of semantic parsing tasks focusing on domains with limited 
access to labelled training data and extend those datasets with synthetically 
generated logical forms.
