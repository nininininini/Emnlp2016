Abstract#==#In this paper we present PaCCSS--IT, a Parallel Corpus of Complex--Simple
Sentences for ITalian. To build the resource we develop a new method for
automatically acquiring a corpus of complex--simple paired sentences able to
intercept structural transformations and particularly suitable for text
simplification. The method requires a wide amount of texts that can be easily
extracted from the web making it suitable also for less--resourced languages.
We test it on the Italian language making available the biggest Italian corpus
for automatic text simplification.
