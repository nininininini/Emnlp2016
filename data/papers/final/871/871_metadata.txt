Abstract#==#We present a neural network architecture to predict a point in 
color space from the sequence of characters in the color's name.  Using large
scale color--name
pairs obtained from an online color design forum, we evaluate our
model on a ``color Turing test'' and find that, given a name,
 the colors predicted by our model are preferred by annotators to color names
created by humans. 
Our datasets and demo system are available online at http://colorlab.us.
