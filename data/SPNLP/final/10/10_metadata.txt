Abstract#==#In this work, we present the first results for neuralizing an Unsupervised
Hidden Markov Model.  We evaluate our approach on tag induction.  Our approach
outperforms existing generative models and is competitive with the
state-of-the-art though with a simpler model easily extended to include
additional context.
